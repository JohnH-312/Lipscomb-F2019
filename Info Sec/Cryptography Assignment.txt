1. Symmetric and Asymmetric Key ALgorithms
Sym- This uses only one key which needs to be shared among all the people who will end up receiving the message. 
Asym- This rather uses a pair of public as well as private key. They are both used in both the process of encryption as well as decryption of messages used in communication.
